üîû Age Restrictions

üö® Adults Only Platform

Meet World is exclusively for users 18 years of age and older. This is strictly enforced for safety and legal compliance.

Why 18+ Only?

  • Safety: Random video chat with strangers poses inherent risks unsuitable for minors
  • Legal Compliance: Adherence to international child protection laws
  • Content Standards: Adult conversations and topics may not be appropriate for younger users
  • Advertising Standards: Compliance with advertiser-friendly content requirements

If you encounter anyone who appears to be under 18, please report them immediately using our reporting system.
