Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Meet World random video chat

+

Is Meet World completely free to use?

Pricing

Yes! Meet World is 100% free to use. There are no hidden fees, premium subscriptions, or paid features. Simply click "Start Chat" and begin connecting with people worldwide at no cost.

+

Do I need to create an account or register?

Privacy

No registration required! Meet World is designed to be completely anonymous. Just visit the site, allow camera access, and start chatting immediately. We don't collect personal information or require accounts.

+

Is my video chat private and secure?

Security

Absolutely! All video and audio connections are peer-to-peer with end-to-end encryption. We don't record, store, or monitor your conversations. Your privacy is completely protected during every chat session.

+

How do I get matched with someone?

Usage

Simply click "Find Someone" in the chat interface and you'll be instantly matched with a random person who's also looking to chat. Our system connects you automatically - no waiting lists or complex matching algorithms.

+

What if I don't like the person I'm matched with?

Usage

No problem! Simply click "Next" to immediately disconnect and get matched with someone new. You have complete control over your conversations and can move on anytime you want.

+

What devices and browsers are supported?

Technical

Meet World works on all modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge) on desktop, mobile, and tablet devices. You just need a camera, microphone, and internet connection to start chatting.

+

Are there any age restrictions?

Safety

Yes, Meet World is for users 18 years and older only. We have strict policies against inappropriate content and maintain community guidelines to ensure a safe environment for all users.

+

What should I do if I encounter inappropriate behavior?

Safety

Immediately click "Next" to disconnect from inappropriate users. For serious violations, please report them to our support team at support@meetworld.social. Meet World has zero tolerance for harassment, illegal content, or abuse.
